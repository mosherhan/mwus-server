FAQ's

How much does a website cost?
+
Our pricing varies depending on the complexity and features required. We offer transparent packages starting from basic sites to full-scale custom solutions. Contact us for a precise quote tailored to your needs.
How does the subscription work?
+
Our subscription model covers maintenance, hosting, and periodic updates to ensure your site remains top-of-the-line. You can choose a monthly or annual plan that fits your strategy.
How do I pause or cancel?
+
You have full control. You can pause or cancel your subscription at any time through your dashboard or by reaching out to our support team. No hidden fees or long-term lock-ins.
How do I communicate with you?
+
We primarily use Slack and email for day-to-day communication, ensuring quick responses and clear logs of all discussions. For major milestones, we schedule Zoom or Google Meet sessions.
What if I don't like the design?
+
We iterate until you're satisfied. Our process includes a discovery phase to align on vision early, and we provide multiple revision cycles to ensure the final product exceeds your expectations.
